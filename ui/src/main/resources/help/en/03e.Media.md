# Media

## Enable Link Preview

Enables a small link preview (thumbnail + description) of most incoming links by Trusted Friends.

Be careful because the link preview is generated on your side, that is, Xeres will connect to the third party site described by the link and fetch the data from it. The third party site will see an incoming connection from you, even if you didn't click on the link. Special care has been taken to use a "generic"
user-agent to avoid making Xeres detection too easy.

This feature only works in private chats (direct, not distant) and only with friends that have their trust level marked as *full* (default is *unknown*).

Default is off. Enable it if you trust your *full* friends, prefer seeing a preview, and end up clicking on most of their links anyway.
